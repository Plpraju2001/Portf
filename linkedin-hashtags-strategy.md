# LinkedIn Hashtag Strategy for Data Science Job Search

## Primary Hashtags (Use 5-7 of these)
These are the most searched by recruiters:

1. **#DataScientist** - Most common job title search
2. **#DataScience** - Broad industry term
3. **#MachineLearning** - High-demand skill
4. **#DataAnalytics** - Popular job category
5. **#OpenToWork** - Critical for job search visibility
6. **#DataScienceJobs** - Direct job search term
7. **#HiringDataScientists** - Recruiters use this

## Secondary Hashtags (Use 3-5 of these)

30. **#ProfessionalDevelopment** - Growth mindset

## Best Practices:

### DO:
✅ Use 20-30 hashtags total (LinkedIn allows up to 3 lines)
✅ Mix primary, secondary, and niche hashtags
✅ Include #OpenToWork if actively job searching
✅ Use location-based hashtags if targeting specific areas (e.g., #DataScienceJobsNYC)
✅ Rotate hashtags between posts to reach different audiences

### DON'T:
❌ Use only generic hashtags
❌ Use hashtags irrelevant to your skills
❌ Overuse the same hashtags in every post
❌ Use too many hashtags (keep it under 30)

## Hashtag Rotation Strategy:

**Post 1 (Certifications):** Focus on #DataScientist #MachineLearning #MicrosoftCertified #OpenToWork
**Post 2 (Project):** Focus on #Python #SQL #DataEngineering #HiringDataScientists
**Post 3 (Blog/Article):** Focus on #DataAnalytics #BusinessIntelligence #DataScienceJobs

This helps you reach different recruiter segments!
